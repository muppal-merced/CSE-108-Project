How to Play

  • Both players guess the same secret 5-letter word.
  • You have 6 attempts and 4 minutes.
  • After each guess, tiles show:
    Green = right letter, right spot
    Yellow = right letter, wrong spot
    Gray = not in word
  • You see your opponent's colors only, not their letters.
  • Winner: First to guess correctly.
  • Both win: Both solve it on the same submit.
  • Both lose: Time runs out or both run out of guesses.
  • If opponent disconnects, you'll see a notification.
